Preparation time


  • Cooking time: 1 hour

  • Prep time: 30 minutes


Ingredients


  • 2 small egg

  • Coconut milk 250g.

  • Pandanus leaf juice 150g.

  • Sugar 150g.

  • Maize flour 3 tablespoon.

  • Salt 1/8 Tablespoon.


How to cook


  1. Blend pandan leaves with water (or pound with a mortar) and strain out the pulp. Prepare pandan water.

  2. Put all the prepared ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth (or knead by hand).

  3. Filter the mixture and set aside.

  4. Put water in a pot and bring to a medium heat. Place a piece of cloth on the bottom of the pot to prevent hot water from erupting when boiling.

  5. Add the rested ingredients and stir continuously until the custard is cooked.

  6. If you can't finish eating it all at once, put it in a jar and boil it in hot water to sterilize it. Store it in the refrigerator for about 3 weeks.
